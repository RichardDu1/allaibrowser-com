## AI-Enhanced Resume Tailoring and Cover Letter Generation

This workflow uses AI to tailor your resume and generate cover letters for each job application. You'll need a base resume, a job description, and access to AI tools.

### Step 1: Prepare Your Base Resume
- Create a comprehensive master resume in a plain text format (e.g., Google Docs). Include all your work experience, skills, education, and achievements.
- Save it as a .txt file for easy copying.

### Step 2: Use Rezi to Tailor Your Resume
- Go to [Rezi](https://www.rezi.ai) and sign up (free tier available).
- Upload your base resume or paste the text.
- Paste the job description into the "Job Description" field.
- Click "Tailor Resume" – Rezi will automatically rewrite bullet points to match keywords and requirements from the job description.
- Review and edit the output. Download the tailored resume as a PDF.

### Step 3: Generate a Cover Letter with Kickresume
- Visit [Kickresume](https://www.kickresume.com) (free tier available).
- Choose a cover letter template.
- Paste the job description and your tailored resume summary.
- Use the AI cover letter generator to create a draft.
- Customize the tone and content to match the company culture.
- Download as PDF.

### Step 4: Refine with ChatGPT
- Open [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) (free tier available).
- Paste the job description and your tailored resume.
- Ask: "Write a compelling cover letter for this job, highlighting my relevant experience."
- Review the output and merge it with the Kickresume draft for a polished version.

### Step 5: Final Review and Submit
- Proofread both documents for errors.
- Save as PDFs with the format: "YourName_CompanyName_Resume.pdf" and "YourName_CompanyName_CoverLetter.pdf".
- Submit via the job portal or email.

### Tips
- Always customize the AI output – add personal anecdotes or specific achievements.
- Use the same keywords from the job description in both documents.
- Repeat this workflow for each application; it takes about 15 minutes per job.
