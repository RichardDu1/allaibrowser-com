## AI-Powered Multi-Platform Content Repurposing for Social Media Managers

### Overview
This workflow leverages AI to repurpose a single piece of content (e.g., a blog post, video transcript, or podcast episode) into platform-specific posts for LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, and Facebook. By using OpenGPT for content generation, Buffer for scheduling, and Canva for visuals, you can maintain a consistent brand voice across platforms while saving hours of manual work.

### Prerequisites
- An OpenGPT account (free tier available)
- A Buffer account (free tier available)
- A Canva account (free tier available)
- Your source content (e.g., blog post URL, transcript, or notes)

### Step 1: Prepare Your Source Content
1. Identify a high-performing piece of content (e.g., a blog post, video, or podcast).
2. Copy the text or key points into a document. If it's a video, use a tool like Otter.ai to get a transcript.
3. Ensure the content is at least 500 words to have enough material for repurposing.

### Step 2: Generate Platform-Specific Posts with OpenGPT
1. Log in to [OpenGPT](https://chatgpt.com) (or use the ChatGPT app).
2. Use the following prompt template to generate posts for each platform:

   **Prompt:**
   ```
   You are a social media content repurposing expert. I have a [type of content] about [topic]. Here is the content: [paste content].

   Please create:
   - 1 LinkedIn post (professional tone, 200-300 words, with a hook, value, and call-to-action)
   - 1 Twitter thread (5-7 tweets, each under 280 characters, with a compelling first tweet)
   - 1 Instagram caption (150-200 words, with emojis, hashtags, and a question to engage)
   - 1 Facebook post (conversational tone, 150-250 words, with a question and link)

   Ensure each post is optimized for its platform and maintains the core message.
   ```

3. Review the generated posts. Edit as needed to match your brand voice.
4. Copy each post into a separate document or note for scheduling.

### Step 3: Create Visuals with Canva
1. Go to [Canva](https://www.canva.com) and create a new design for each platform:
   - LinkedIn: 1200 x 627 px
   - Twitter: 1200 x 675 px
   - Instagram: 1080 x 1080 px (square) or 1080 x 1350 px (portrait)
   - Facebook: 1200 x 630 px
2. Use Canva's AI-powered Magic Design: upload your source content or a key image, and let AI suggest templates.
3. Customize the design with your brand colors, logo, and text overlay (e.g., a quote from the post).
4. Download each image as PNG or JPG.

### Step 4: Schedule Posts with Buffer
1. Log in to [Buffer](https://buffer.com).
2. Connect your LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, and Facebook accounts.
3. Create a new post for each platform:
   - Paste the corresponding text from Step 2.
   - Upload the visual from Step 3.
   - Add relevant hashtags (e.g., #ContentMarketing #AI).
4. Set the optimal posting times (e.g., LinkedIn: Tue-Thu 8-10 AM, Twitter: 12-1 PM, Instagram: 11 AM-2 PM, Facebook: 1-4 PM).
5. Schedule the posts to go out over the next week (one per day to avoid overwhelming your audience).

### Step 5: Monitor and Iterate
1. Use Buffer's analytics to track engagement (likes, comments, shares, clicks).
2. After one week, identify which platform performed best.
3. Adjust your prompts in OpenGPT for future repurposing based on what resonated (e.g., more questions for Instagram, more data for LinkedIn).
4. Repeat the workflow weekly with new source content.

### Tips for Success
- **Batch process:** Repurpose 3-5 pieces of content in one sitting to maximize efficiency.
- **Maintain consistency:** Use the same brand voice and visual style across platforms.
- **Leverage AI for variations:** Ask OpenGPT to generate multiple versions of a post (e.g., A/B test headlines).
- **Respect platform norms:** LinkedIn favors long-form, professional content; Twitter thrives on brevity and threads; Instagram is visual and emotional; Facebook is conversational.

### Troubleshooting
- **AI output too generic?** Add more context to your prompt (e.g., target audience, brand guidelines).
- **Visuals not matching?** Use Canva's Brand Kit to save colors and fonts.
- **Scheduling conflicts?** Use Buffer's queue to auto-fill gaps.

By following this workflow, you can repurpose one piece of content into four platform-specific posts in under 30 minutes, freeing up time for strategy and engagement.
