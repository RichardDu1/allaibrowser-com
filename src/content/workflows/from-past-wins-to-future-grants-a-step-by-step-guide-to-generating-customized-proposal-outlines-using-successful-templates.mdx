## Overview

This workflow teaches nonprofit grant writers how to build a reusable library of successful proposal sections and use AI to generate customized outlines for new grant opportunities. By analyzing past winning proposals, you can identify patterns, key phrases, and structures that resonate with funders, then apply them to new contexts.

## Prerequisites

- Access to at least 3-5 past successful grant proposals (your own or from similar organizations)
- A Google Drive account (free)
- A ChatGPT account (free tier works; Plus recommended for longer outputs)
- A Notion account (free) for organizing your library

## Step 1: Collect and Organize Past Successful Proposals

1. **Gather your winning proposals**: Locate all grant proposals that were funded in the last 3 years. Aim for at least 5 examples.
2. **Upload to Google Drive**: Create a folder named "Successful Proposals Library" and upload PDFs or Docs.
3. **Create a master spreadsheet**: In Google Sheets, list each proposal with columns: Funder Name, Grant Amount, Program Area, Key Outcomes, and Unique Selling Points.

## Step 2: Analyze and Extract Reusable Components

1. **Identify common sections**: Most proposals have: Executive Summary, Problem Statement, Solution, Goals & Objectives, Methods, Evaluation, Budget Narrative, and Organizational Capacity.
2. **Extract high-impact sentences**: For each section, copy 2-3 sentences that clearly articulate your value. Store these in a Notion database with tags (e.g., "Problem Statement", "Data-Driven").
3. **Note the structure**: Observe how each proposal flows. For example, does it start with a story or data? Use this to create a template.

## Step 3: Build a Prompt Template for ChatGPT

Create a reusable prompt that generates a customized outline. Here's a sample:

```
You are an expert grant writer for a nonprofit. Based on the following successful proposal components, generate a detailed outline for a new grant proposal targeting [Funder Name] for [Program Name]. The outline should include each section with bullet points for key content, and incorporate language from the successful examples.

Successful Components:
- Problem Statement: [paste 2-3 sentences]
- Solution: [paste 2-3 sentences]
- Goals & Objectives: [paste 2-3 sentences]
- Evaluation: [paste 2-3 sentences]

New Grant Details:
- Funder: [name]
- Program: [name]
- Target Population: [description]
- Geographic Area: [region]
- Funding Amount: [amount]

Please produce a comprehensive outline.
```

## Step 4: Generate the Outline

1. **Copy the prompt** into ChatGPT.
2. **Fill in the placeholders** with your new grant details and the extracted components.
3. **Review the output**: ChatGPT will generate a structured outline. It may include suggested headings, bullet points, and even sample sentences.
4. **Refine as needed**: Ask ChatGPT to adjust tone, add more detail, or focus on specific sections.

## Step 5: Customize and Save

1. **Transfer the outline** to a new Google Doc or Notion page.
2. **Tailor the content**: Replace generic phrases with your organization's specific data, stories, and metrics.
3. **Save the final outline** in a folder named "Proposal Outlines" for future reference.

## Step 6: Iterate and Improve

- After submitting the proposal, track its outcome. If funded, add it to your successful library.
- Periodically update your extracted components with new winning language.
- Experiment with different prompt styles to see what yields the best outlines.

## Tips for Success

- **Always verify facts**: AI can hallucinate data. Double-check statistics and quotes.
- **Maintain privacy**: Remove sensitive information from prompts (e.g., specific financials).
- **Combine with human expertise**: Use AI as a starting point, then inject your unique organizational voice.

## Troubleshooting

- **Outline too generic?** Add more specific examples from your past proposals.
- **Missing sections?** Explicitly request them in the prompt (e.g., "Include a section on sustainability").
- **AI repeats itself?** Ask for a different structure or provide more varied examples.

## Conclusion

By systematizing your past successes and leveraging AI, you can dramatically reduce the time spent on proposal outlines while maintaining high quality. This workflow turns your institutional knowledge into a scalable asset.
