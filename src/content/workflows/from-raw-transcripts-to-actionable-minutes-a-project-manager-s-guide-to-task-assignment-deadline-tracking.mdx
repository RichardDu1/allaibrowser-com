## Transforming Raw Meeting Transcripts into Actionable Minutes with Assigned Tasks and Deadlines

### Overview
This workflow automates the conversion of raw meeting transcripts into structured minutes with assigned tasks and deadlines. Using Otter.ai for transcription and Notion for task management, you'll reduce manual effort and ensure accountability.

### Prerequisites
- Otter.ai account (free or Business plan)
- Notion account (free or Team plan)
- Meeting recordings or live transcription access

### Step 1: Capture the Transcript
1. **Use Otter.ai** to record and transcribe your meeting in real-time or upload an audio file.
   - For live meetings: Start Otter.ai and invite the bot to your meeting (supports Zoom, Google Meet, Teams).
   - For recordings: Upload the audio file to Otter.ai.
2. **Review the transcript** for accuracy. Otter.ai automatically identifies speakers and timestamps.

### Step 2: Extract Key Information
1. **Open the transcript** in Otter.ai.
2. **Use the 'Highlight' feature** to mark action items, decisions, and key points.
3. **Export the transcript** as a .txt or .docx file (or use Otter.ai's 'Summary' feature for a condensed version).

### Step 3: Structure the Minutes in Notion
1. **Create a new page** in Notion with a template for meeting minutes. Include sections:
   - Meeting Title, Date, Attendees
   - Agenda Items
   - Decisions Made
   - Action Items (with assignee and deadline)
2. **Paste the transcript** or summary into the relevant sections.
3. **Use Notion AI** (optional) to summarize or reformat the content.

### Step 4: Assign Tasks and Deadlines
1. **For each action item**, create a task in Notion using the '/task' command or a database.
2. **Assign the task** to a team member using the 'Assignee' property.
3. **Set a deadline** using the 'Date' property.
4. **Add tags** like 'High Priority' or 'Meeting Follow-up'.

### Step 5: Automate with Zapier (Optional)
1. **Create a Zap** that triggers when a new Otter.ai transcript is ready.
2. **Send the transcript** to Notion as a new page with pre-filled properties.
3. **Use AI parsing** (e.g., GPT-4 via Zapier) to extract action items and create tasks automatically.

### Step 6: Distribute and Track
1. **Share the Notion page** with your team via a link or email.
2. **Use Notion's calendar view** to track deadlines.
3. **Set reminders** for upcoming due dates.

### Tips for Success
- **Standardize templates** in Notion for consistency.
- **Review minutes** within 24 hours while details are fresh.
- **Integrate with Slack** for real-time notifications.

### Troubleshooting
- **Poor transcription quality**: Use Otter.ai's 'Custom Vocabulary' for industry terms.
- **Missing action items**: Manually review the transcript for implicit tasks.

### Conclusion
This workflow turns hours of manual note-taking into a streamlined process. By leveraging Otter.ai and Notion, you'll produce actionable minutes that drive project progress.
